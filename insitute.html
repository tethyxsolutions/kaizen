Diploma in Practical Accounting and Taxation (DPAT)

DPTA is a professional course in practical accounting and taxation. This course divided in to two stages Stage 1Preliminary (2 Month)At this preliminary stage we give you in depth knowledge in the field of Accounts and Taxation like GST, INCOME TAX, COMPANY LAW, ACCOUNTING STANDARDS, ESI, PF and basic knowledge in all other related business and commercial laws. At this stage we will teach you the both the theoretical as well as the practical application of each topic. Stage 2 Advanced (6 month) At this advanced stage we will place you in a enterprise at your convenience. You can apply all the practical aspects in real life experience. You work as an independent accountant, and at the same time we will guide you in all aspect of your work. We will hook you to be a professional Accountant. You can earn a minimum of Rs 5000/- per month during this stage After successful completion of both the stages you will place you in a good firm, and you can expect a good salary as per the industrial standard.
